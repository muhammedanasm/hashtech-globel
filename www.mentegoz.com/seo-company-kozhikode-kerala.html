On Page SEO

On-Page SEO involves optimizing elements directly on a website to improve search engine rankings. This includes optimizing meta tags, content, and internal linking for better visibility and user experience.

Off Page SEO

Off-Page SEO involves strategies outside of a website to boost search engine rankings. This includes link building and social media marketing to increase website authority and visibility.

Technical SEO

Technical SEO optimizes website performance for better search engine visibility. Tasks include improving site speed, fixing broken links, and implementing structured data markup.

Link Building

Link building is essential for SEO, involving acquiring links from other websites. These backlinks boost authority and improve search engine rankings, driving organic traffic and enhancing online visibility.

Keyword Research

Keyword research is crucial for SEO, involving identifying and analyzing search terms. This informs content creation and optimization efforts to improve search engine rankings and attract relevant traffic.

Local SEO

Local SEO targets local customers by optimizing online presence. This includes Google My Business and location-based keywords to improve local search rankings and drive foot traffic.
